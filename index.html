about us

P I E K L E INTERNATIONAL LIMITED

P I E K L E International Ltd. is a multidisciplinary company specializing in Mechanical, Civil, and Electrical engineering. Established in 1990, with its headquarters in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, the company is actively involved in design, construction, and turnkey projects, showcasing their expertise and versatility.

P I E K L E International Ltd. has a proven track record of successfully executing projects in diverse Nigerian locations, including Abuja, Lagos, Calabar, Port Harcourt, and Anambra State. With a skilled workforce and advanced resources, we are well-equipped to handle civil, structural, mechanical, and electrical projects, including electrical work, plumbing, telecommunication, and other associated services in building projects.

Our services

Our team comprises professionals such as Engineers, Land Surveyors, Quantity Surveyors, Architects, Builders, Technicians, and administrative staff.
